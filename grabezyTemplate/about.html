What Is Grabezy? Short DIscription!

Grabezy is an easy to-use, powerful, Open Site online store management that can sell multiple online products from a single cart. Customer are simply by filling in forms and checking out and by paying amount can order various products from Grabezy. There are many professionally catagory available to customize the cart to your needs. An Grabezy store can be ready to take orders soon after checking out. All you have to order is have it available for you, select a producct from the many catagory and order from the site, and your product descriptions and photos, click a few settings, and you are ready to begin accepting orders. Our free delivery & Quick Start chapters show you how. The public side or “Storefront” of Grabezy looks very professional and ready to sell almost as soon as it is ordered.
